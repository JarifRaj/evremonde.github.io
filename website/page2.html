entry one
JOURNAL

I, Marquis Saint Evremonde, have recently resolved to keep a digitized diary.
I consign the duty of upkeeping the digitization to my trusted nephew,
Charles Evremonde, once I grow unable. I am convinced the great historians of the future
would benefit in recollecting my opulency; as well as my numerous contributions to mankind.
